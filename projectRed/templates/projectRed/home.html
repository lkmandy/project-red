Why November?

November is a busy month on campus: classes are in full swing, days are shorter, and many students are starting to worry about holiday costs. Project Red runs in November so support can reach families before the winter break.

Who we support

Project Red focuses on Assiniboine students and their households. Support can look like a holiday food hamper for local students, or grocery gift cards for students who study remotely or can’t easily get to campus.

How you can help

Students, staff, and community members can join the November campaign by spreading the word, organizing mini-drives, or asking how to donate non-perishable items and gift cards.
